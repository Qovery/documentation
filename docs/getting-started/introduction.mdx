## Welcome to Qovery

Qovery is the **Kubernetes control plane for humans and AI agents** — running on your own AWS, GCP, Azure, or Scaleway.

Describe what you want to deploy. An AI agent handles the rest. Or use the console, CLI, Terraform, or API. Everything drives the same control plane.
